Our History

OLAOLUWA OLABIYI a counselling psychologist was born in Oyo town Oyo state to the family of MR & MRS AWOLOWO in the 90's, and had his primary education at foundation model nursery and primary school Oyo and secondary education at EACOED Model High school Oyo in the year 2013. Due to his high level of intelligence, he got admitted into the UNIVERSITY OF IBADAN  the same year.

He Was admitted into the department of GUIDANCE & COUNSELLING. where he was for four years for his bachelor degree in education guidance and counselling. During the course of the four year program he had remarkable and ground breaking record to himself which fetch him who he is today.
